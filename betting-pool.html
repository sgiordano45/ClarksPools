Starting Bankroll

  • Everyone starts with $1,000 in fake money
  • Goal is to have the highest bankroll at the end of the playoffs
  • No real money is wagered on individual bets
  • Pool buy-in is separate from betting bankroll

Bet Types Allowed

  • Spreads: Bet on a team to cover the point spread
  • Moneylines: Bet on a team to win straight up
  • No parlays allowed
  • No player props allowed
  • No over/under totals

Betting Limits

  • Minimum bet: $10
  • Maximum bet: $500 per wager
  • No limit on number of bets per round
  • Cannot bet more than your current bankroll

Bet Visibility

  • All bets are private until kickoff
  • Bets become visible to all players once the first game of the round kicks off
  • This prevents copying other players' picks

Locking Schedule

  • All bets for a round lock at kickoff of the FIRST game
  • You cannot modify or cancel bets after lock
  • Lines may change up until lock time
  • Your bet uses the line at time of placement
‚ö†Ô∏è Even if a game is on Sunday, bets lock Saturday when the first game kicks off!

Payouts & Winning

  • Standard -110 odds pay out ~$91 on a $100 bet
  • Underdog moneylines pay according to listed odds
  • Pushes return original wager
  • Highest bankroll after Super Bowl wins the pool
