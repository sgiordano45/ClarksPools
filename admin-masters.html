Recalculate Scores from ESPN Leaderboard

      

        Fetches the current Masters leaderboard from ESPN, computes each entry's total score
        relative to par (best 6 of 9 golfers count; MC/WD players receive worst R3 to-par + worst R4 to-par
        as their penalty and only fill slots needed to reach 6 scorers). Lowest score wins.
        Writes totalScore back to every entry.
